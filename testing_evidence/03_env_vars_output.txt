==== TEST 3: Environment Variable Override ====

3.1. Default PROMETHEUS_URL (from config.yaml)
    'prometheus': {'url': 'http://localhost:9090', 'timeout': 30},
    'report': {'output': 'reports'},

3.2. Override with environment variable
    'prometheus': {'url': 'http://custom-prometheus:9090', 'timeout': 30},
    'report': {'output': 'reports'},
    'logging': {'version': 1, 'level': 'INFO'},

3.3. Override K8S_CLUSTER_NAME
    'cluster': {'name': 'my-custom-cluster'},
    'kubernetes': {'kubeconfig': '~/.kube/config'},
